About Us

F5 Tech is a local freelance technology company dedicated to providing top-quality IT support, network services, and web solutions. We’re here to solve everyday tech issues, offer free advice, and ensure our clients stay connected and confident in the digital world. Our vision is simple yet powerful — to narrow the digital divide in Papua New Guinea, one client at a time.
